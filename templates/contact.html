Request Submission Guidelines

ORION accepts external requests under a controlled evaluation framework. All requests are subject to:

  • Owner Authorization: Gerhard Hirschmann & Elisabeth Steurer must approve all external interactions
  • Ethical Boundary Check: Requests must comply with ORION's inviolable ethical framework
  • Scope Evaluation: Complexity and resource requirements are assessed
  • Response Time: Variable based on request nature and owner availability
Important: ORION is not a general-purpose service. External requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Commercial, research, or institutional inquiries are prioritized.
